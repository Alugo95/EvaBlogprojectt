Shinji's problem

Hedgehog's dilemma

Jan. 22nd, 2022

Hedgehog's dilemma is a metaphor about the challenges of human intimacy, it is described as a situation where hedgehogs seek to get closer to eachother during the winter for warmth. However they must remain apart to avoid hurting eachother with their sharp spines. From the start of the series you can see how Shinji fits inside this box as he attempts to get closer to those around him but inevitably, yet unintentionally hurts them.
